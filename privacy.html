Booking & Cancellation Policies

HyFlow Massage LLC · Mountain View, CA · Effective April 2026

💳
Full Prepayment Required

All appointments at HyFlow Massage LLC require full payment at the time of booking. Your appointment is not confirmed until payment has been received.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets via Square's secure payment system. Gift cards are also accepted.

Why prepayment? Prepayment ensures we can dedicate our full attention and preparation to your session, and protects both your time and ours.
📅

Notice Given Refund Fee Charged
More than 24 hours before appointment 100% Full Refund No charge
Less than 24 hours before appointment 50% Refund 50% of service price
Same day cancellation No Refund 100% of service price
No-show (no notice) No Refund 100% of service price

To cancel your appointment, please contact us at (408) 668-6562 or email booking@hyflowmassage.com as soon as possible.

💰
Refund Policy

Eligible refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

If HyFlow Massage LLC cancels or reschedules your appointment for any reason, you will receive a full refund or the option to reschedule at no additional cost.

Emergency exceptions: We understand that genuine emergencies happen. If you experience a medical emergency or serious unforeseen circumstance, please contact us directly and we will review your situation on a case-by-case basis.
⏰
Late Arrivals

Please arrive 5 minutes early for your appointment. If you arrive late, your session will end at the originally scheduled time and the full session fee applies.

If you are running late, please call or text (408) 668-6562 to let us know.

🏥
Post-Surgical Clients

Clients booking Post-Surgery Lymphatic Drainage services must provide written physician clearance prior to their first session. Appointments cannot be confirmed without this documentation.

Timing of post-surgical massage varies by surgery type — please consult your surgeon. General guidelines: cosmetic surgery (3–7 days post-op), abdominal surgery (4–6 weeks post-op).

🎁
Gift Card Policy

HyFlow Massage LLC gift cards never expire and are fully transferable. Gift cards are redeemable for any service.

Gift cards are non-refundable once purchased. Lost or stolen gift cards cannot be replaced.

🔒
Privacy Policy

HyFlow Massage LLC collects only the information necessary to provide our services — your name, contact information, and health intake details. This information is kept strictly confidential and is never sold or shared with third parties.

Payment information is processed securely through Square and is never stored on our systems.

For questions about your personal information, contact us at booking@hyflowmassage.com.

Questions? Call or text (408) 668-6562 or email booking@hyflowmassage.com
