Lymphatic Drainage Massage

Mountain View's Premier Manual Lymphatic Drainage · MLD Specialist · Bay Area

Lymphatic drainage massage (LDM) is a clinically precise, evidence-based therapeutic technique that works with your body's lymphatic system to reduce swelling, eliminate toxins, and accelerate recovery. At HyFlow Massage LLC, our therapists are trained in Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD) — the gold-standard technique developed by Dr. Emil Vodder.

Using light, rhythmic, directional strokes that follow the exact pathways of the lymphatic system, we stimulate lymph flow, reduce fluid retention, and activate the body's natural detoxification process. Most clients feel noticeably lighter and less swollen within 24 hours.

Who Is This For?

✦  Post-surgical recovery — BBL, lipo, tummy tuck
✦  Lymphedema management
✦  Chronic swelling — legs, arms, face
✦  Bloating and water retention
✦  Immune system support and detox
✦  Skin health — acne, rosacea, puffiness

Why Choose HyFlow for LDM?

  • ✦Medical clearance protocol for all post-surgical clients

  • ✦Also available as mobile — we come to you during recovery

Thai Massage

Traditional Thai Massage Mountain View · Flexibility & Energy · Bay Area

Thai massage is a 2,500-year-old healing art that combines acupressure, assisted yoga stretching, and rhythmic compression along the body's energy lines (sen lines). Unlike table massage, Thai massage is performed on a comfortable floor mat with the client fully clothed — allowing for deep joint mobilization and full-body stretch sequences not possible on a table.

At HyFlow Massage LLC, our Thai massage therapists are trained in traditional Northern Thai techniques, delivering a session that simultaneously improves flexibility, restores energy flow, releases tension, and leaves you feeling both deeply relaxed and energized.

Key Benefits

  • ✦Dramatically improves flexibility and range of motion in hips, spine, and shoulders
  • ✦Relieves joint stiffness — knees, hips, lower back, neck
  • ✦Stimulates sen energy lines — restores balance and vitality
  • ✦No oils needed — suitable for all skin types, no undressing required
  • ✦Energizing yet deeply relaxing — unique combination found in no other modality

Why Choose HyFlow for Thai Massage?
