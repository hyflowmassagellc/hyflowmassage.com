It depends on your surgery type:

• Cosmetic surgery (liposuction, plastic surgery): Often 3–7 days post-op once the wound is closed. Many surgeons recommend starting within 48–72 hours for early gentle drainage.

• Cancer/tumor surgery (lymph node removal): Usually 2–4 weeks after drains are removed and incision is stable. Must be performed by a therapist with oncology rehab credentials.

• Abdominal/pelvic surgery (C-section, hysterectomy): Generally 4–6 weeks after physician confirms internal healing.

Requirements before starting: Wound must be closed with no open incisions, no acute infection or fever, and physician clearance is required. We ask all post-surgical clients to provide written medical clearance (doctor's note) before booking — this protects both you and your therapist.
